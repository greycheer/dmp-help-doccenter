●**Copy Product: **When copying a product, associated apps must be re-selected. The Primary App prioritizes alignment with the original product's Primary App; if the original Primary App is not selected, the first selected app is used by default. ●**Import/Export: **Product import/export involves the associated app field and must be handled synchronously. ●**Front-End Categories: **Front-end categories are isolated by app. Initialization data is defined by the product team via Excel.
