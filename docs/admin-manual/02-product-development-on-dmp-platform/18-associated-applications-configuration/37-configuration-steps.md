**Step 1: Select Associated Applications**
In the "Associated Application" area, check the apps you want to associate with this product. Associated apps appear in the "Associated Application" list, and available apps are shown in the "Available Associated Application" area.
NOTE: If the number of associated apps is 0, devices under this PID cannot be discovered or added by any app.
**Step 2: Set Primary App**
When a product is associated with multiple apps, click "Set Primary APP" next to one of them. That app will then display the "Primary App" label.
●Rule: If only 1 app is associated, it automatically becomes the Primary App without manual selection. ●If multiple apps are associated, exactly one must be designated as the Primary App. ●Changes must be made while the product is in "In Development" status. Click "Save and Update", then switch to "Pre-Release" for changes to take effect.
#### 
**Step 3: Submit and Publish**
After configuration, click "Save and Update", then switch the product status to "Pre-Release". The association relationship will take effect. The product status button is located at the top of the page, next to the product info.

**Step 4: View Detailed Rules**
Click "Details>" in the "Feature Overview" panel on the right side of the page to open the complete rules popup.
